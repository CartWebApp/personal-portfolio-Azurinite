Case Study 03

EECU Budget Calculator

March 16th, 2025 View Case Study

Clients requested a financial literacy web application which would help young adults transitioning to the workspace plan how to spend their budget.

Users would receive an approximation of the income they make, then fill out expenses per category to find out whether they have a “surplus” or “shortfall” of money.

Research Paper

Synthesized from three sources and a personally conducted interview with a zoo specialist to propose effective ways for integrating technology into the enrichments of animals requiring greater cognitive abilities such as primates and elephants.

CART Showcase Reflection

September 22nd, 2025 See More

CART, the facility at which I began my coding journey, hosted its annual Showcase event, in which we could visit other labs and see the work they do.
